## 💡 Quick Reference: Current BD Market Status
*Keep these key points in mind when reading alumni responses:*

* **General (O-General):** Distributed by **Esquire Electronics**. They do **not** officially sell a wall-mounted inverter with Hot & Cool. Any seller offering one is either selling a parallel import (no official warranty, hard to find parts) or a counterfeit.
* **Mitsubishi Heavy Industries (MHI):** Distributed by **Esquire Electronics**. Extremely durable ("Heavy Duty"). They have Hot & Cool inverter split units globally, but they are rarely stocked in Dhaka. You can contact Esquire directly to check if they can special-order one.
* **Daikin:** Distributed by **Transcom Digital** & Shamrock/Shamim Electronics. World-class efficiency, but heat pumps are rare in retail stock. Check if Transcom can source a model like the FTHT series.
* **Gree:** Distributed by **Electro Mart**. Their **Clivia** and **Fairy** Inverter Hot & Cool series are widely available, highly reliable, and come with a 10-year compressor warranty. 
* **Haier:** Distributed directly by **Haier Bangladesh**. Very reliable, features self-cleaning technology, and offers excellent customer service.
