## 🚨 Critical Market Warning for General (O-General) in Bangladesh

General (Fujitsu General) is one of the most premium and durable AC brands in Bangladesh. However, because of its popularity, it is also the **most heavily counterfeited brand** in the country. Many non-authorized shops in Stadium Market, Hatirpool, or online marketplaces sell cheap, locally assembled ACs with fake General stickers.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Esquire Electronics Limited** is the **sole authorized distributor** of General (Fujitsu General) in Bangladesh. 
> To guarantee authenticity, official warranty, and genuine spare parts, you should buy either directly from an **Esquire Electronics Showroom** or an authorized dealer verified on their website.

## 🔍 The General Brand Dilemma: Inverter + Wall-Mounted + Hot & Cool

Before you visit a showroom, you must understand a key limitation in General's official product lineup in Bangladesh:

1. **Wall-Mounted Inverter Models (Cooling-Only):** The official wall-mounted inverter models (the **ASGG series**, e.g., `ASGG-12CPTA-V`, `ASGG-18CPTA-V`, `ASGG-24CPTA-V`) are **Cooling-Only** systems. They do not have heating capability.
2. **Wall-Mounted Hot & Cool Models (Non-Inverter):** General offers wall-mounted Hot & Cool units (the **ASG series**, e.g., `ASG-18RBAJ`, `ASG-24RBAJ`), but these are **non-inverter** (constant speed) models.
3. **Inverter Hot & Cool Models (Ceiling/Cassette):** The only official General inverter models with heating (Hot & Cool) are ceiling-type/cassette units (e.g., `ABHG-18KRTA`, `ABHG-30KRTA`). These are expensive, require false ceilings, and are typically used in commercial settings or very large rooms.

> [!WARNING]
> If a local retailer offers you a **"Wall-Mounted General Inverter Hot & Cool AC"**:
> - It is highly likely a **counterfeit/fake** product.
> - Or it is a **parallel import (grey market)** unit brought in from another country. These units do not carry any official Esquire Electronics warranty, and finding spare parts or repair support in Dhaka will be extremely difficult.
